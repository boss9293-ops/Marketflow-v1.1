## Landing Page Section Draft

## Semiconductor Intelligence

SOXX does not move as one simple block. At different points in the cycle, AI compute, memory, equipment, foundry exposure, and the rest of SOXX can participate very differently.

Semiconductor Intelligence separates that structure.

The SOXX/SOXL Lens uses SOXX holdings, selected bucket contribution, residual participation, and SOXX-relative strength to show whether movement is concentrated or broadening. For SOXL users, the Lens adds daily sensitivity context without treating SOXL as a simple multi-day 3x forecast.

A separate AI Infrastructure Radar monitors broader AI infrastructure themes such as power, grid equipment, cooling, cloud CAPEX, and nuclear/SMR. These themes may not directly contribute to SOXX today, but they can help users track where AI capital pressure may be forming next.

Historical structure context only. Not a forecast, recommendation, or trading signal.
