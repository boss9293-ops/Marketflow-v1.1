# WO-VR-01 — VR Survival Lab Positioning

Version: v0.1

## Purpose
Reposition VR as a Survival Lab for crash-response strategy research and simulation, not as a real-time signal engine.

## Current Positioning
- Standard = risk detection and live market context.
- VR = response strategy research and survival simulation.
- Playback = historical evidence.
- Backtest = survival strategy comparison.
- Avoid language that implies real-time buy/sell triggers, crash calls, or a separate risk alarm source.
