# Semiconductor Engine — Demo Walkthrough Script

---

## English Version

### Opening

> "This engine tracks AI-era semiconductor capital flow using SOXX as the anchor. Let me walk you through how to read it."

---

### Step 1 — AI Regime

> "First, look at the AI Regime label in the top strip. This tells us the current structure at a glance — whether AI leadership is broad, narrow, rotating, recovering, or contracting. Today it shows [regime label]. That means [regime explanation]."

AI Regime interpretations:
- **AI-led Broadening** → "AI leadership is confirmed across multiple semiconductor segments — the broadest structural confirmation."
- **Narrow AI Leadership** → "AI Compute is leading, but other segments have not yet followed — concentration risk is elevated."
- **Capital Rotation** → "Capital is moving from AI infrastructure toward equipment and foundry — the structure is shifting."
- **Broad Recovery** → "Participation is recovering broadly across all segments — an early-cycle confirmation."
- **Semiconductor Contraction** → "Structural weakness is confirmed across all segments."

---

### Step 2 — Relative Spread

> "Next, look at the Relative Spread chart [CYCLE VIEW 1]. Each line shows how much stronger or weaker that bucket is compared to SOXX. Lines above zero are supporting SOXX. Lines below zero are lagging. Right now, [AI Compute / Memory / Foundry / Equipment] is [leading / lagging]."

---

### Step 3 — Rebased Flow

> "Then, look at the Rebased Bucket Flow [CYCLE VIEW 2]. All series start at zero from the same date. You can see which group moved first and which groups followed. [AI Compute pulled ahead early / All buckets moved together / Memory and Equipment are lagging behind]."

---

### Step 4 — Capital Flow Stage

> "The stage timeline [CYCLE VIEW 3] shows how far AI-related capital has spread across the semiconductor value chain. The flow goes: AI Compute → Memory → Foundry → Equipment → Broad Participation. Today's reading shows [confirmed / partial / mixed / lagging] at each stage."

---

### Step 5 — SOXL Sensitivity

> "Finally, SOXL sensitivity [right panel] shows how the current SOXX structure may be amplified in the leveraged ETF. Today's reading is [High / Medium / Low–Medium], meaning [narrow leadership amplifies risk / rotation creates uneven amplification / broad participation is being amplified]."

---

### Closing

> "This is not a trading signal and not a forecast. It is a structural reference tool for understanding where AI-era semiconductor capital is flowing and how far it has spread."

## Korean Version

### 오프닝

> "이 엔진은 SOXX를 기준으로 AI 시대 반도체 자본 흐름을 추적합니다. 읽는 방법을 설명드리겠습니다."

---

### Step 1 — AI 레짐

> "먼저, 상단 KPI 영역의 AI Regime 레이블을 확인합니다. 현재 구조를 한눈에 보여줍니다 — AI 리더십이 광범위한지, 집중되어 있는지, 순환 중인지, 회복 중인지, 또는 수축 중인지. 현재는 [레짐 레이블]을 표시하고 있습니다."

---

### Step 2 — 상대 스프레드

> "다음으로 상대 스프레드 차트를 봅니다. 각 선은 해당 버킷이 SOXX보다 얼마나 강하거나 약한지를 보여줍니다. 0선 위는 SOXX를 지지하는 그룹, 0선 아래는 SOXX에 뒤처지는 그룹입니다."

---

### Step 3 — 리베이스 흐름

> "리베이스 버킷 흐름 차트를 보면, 같은 출발점에서 어떤 그룹이 먼저 움직였고 어떤 그룹이 따라왔는지 확인할 수 있습니다."

---

### Step 4 — 자본 흐름 단계

> "자본 흐름 단계 타임라인은 AI 자본이 반도체 밸류체인 전반에 얼마나 확산되었는지 보여줍니다. 흐름: AI 컴퓨팅 → 메모리 → 파운드리 → 장비 → 광범위한 참여."

---

### Step 5 — SOXL 민감도

> "마지막으로 SOXL 민감도는 현재 SOXX 구조가 레버리지 ETF에서 얼마나 증폭될 수 있는지 보여줍니다. 현재 [High / Medium / Low–Medium]으로 표시됩니다."
