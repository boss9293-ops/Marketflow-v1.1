# AI Regime Lens for Semiconductor Capital Flow
**AI 레짐 기반 반도체 자본 흐름 분석**

---

## 1. What This Engine Does

This engine tracks AI-era semiconductor capital flow using SOXX as the anchor.

It shows which semiconductor groups support or weaken SOXX, and how capital spreads from AI Compute into Memory, Foundry, Equipment, and broader participation.

**Korean:**
이 엔진은 SOXX를 기준으로 AI 시대 반도체 자본 흐름을 추적합니다.
어느 그룹이 SOXX를 지지하거나 약화시키는지, 자본이 AI 컴퓨팅에서 메모리, 파운드리, 장비, 광범위한 참여로 어떻게 확산되는지를 보여줍니다.

---

## 2. Why SOXX Is the Anchor

SOXX (iShares Semiconductor ETF) represents the broad semiconductor market and provides the base for comparing each AI semiconductor bucket.

When a bucket line is above SOXX, that group is outperforming the broader semiconductor market. When below, it is lagging.

**Korean:**
SOXX는 반도체 시장 전체를 대표하는 기준축이며, 각 AI 반도체 버킷의 상대강도를 비교하는 기준으로 사용됩니다.

## 3. What the Buckets Mean

| Bucket | Tickers | Role |
|--------|---------|------|
| **AI Compute** | NVDA, AMD, AVGO | AI GPU and networking silicon — the primary driver in an AI-led cycle |
| **Memory** | MU (+ Samsung / SK Hynix when available) | DRAM and HBM capacity supporting AI workloads |
| **Foundry / Packaging** | TSM | Manufacturing capacity for advanced semiconductor nodes |
| **Equipment** | ASML, AMAT, LRCX, KLAC | Tools enabling fab production — typically the lagging confirmation stage |

In a healthy AI-led cycle, capital flows from AI Compute → Memory → Foundry → Equipment → Broad Participation.

| 장비 | 팹 생산을 가능하게 하는 도구 — 통상 후행 확인 단계 |

## 4. How to Read the Charts

### Relative Spread vs SOXX
Shows which semiconductor groups are stronger or weaker than SOXX at any point in time.

- Line **above zero** → bucket outperforming SOXX (supporting the structure)
- Line **below zero** → bucket underperforming SOXX (weakening the structure)
- Zero line = SOXX baseline

### Rebased Bucket Flow
All series start at zero from the selected window start. Shows which group moved first and what followed.

- Separation between AI Compute and others → narrow AI leadership
- All series moving together → broad semiconductor participation

### Capital Flow Stage
Shows how far AI-related capital has confirmed across the semiconductor value chain.

| Stage | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Confirmed | Bucket clearly outperforming SOXX (+3pp or more) |
| Partial | Moderate outperformance |
| Mixed | Near SOXX baseline |
| Lagging / Weak | Underperforming SOXX |

## 5. What SOXL Sensitivity Means

SOXL is a 3× leveraged ETF tracking the PHLX Semiconductor Index.

SOXL Sensitivity shows how the current SOXX structure may become more amplified in a leveraged instrument.

| Sensitivity | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| High | Narrow leadership or contraction — leverage amplifies risk |
| Medium | Rotation or partial recovery — uneven structure |
| Low–Medium | Broad AI leadership — leverage amplifies broad participation |

**Korean:**
SOXL 민감도는 현재 SOXX 구조가 레버리지 ETF에서 얼마나 크게 반영될 수 있는지를 보여줍니다.
